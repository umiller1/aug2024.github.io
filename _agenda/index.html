Fun Fact

– Butterflies and Moths are opportunistic blood-drinkers and tear drinkers. This means that they do not bite or seek out blood or tears, rather, if they happen to come across some, they will drink it to gain nutrients. This is referred to as mud-puddling, where they do the same for sweat, excrement, rotting plants, rain puddles, etc.
